- Base.
When winning a Clash with a Blunt Base Attack Skill, inflict 2 Defense Level Down next turn against the target. (once per turn for each Identity; effect cannot stack against the same enemy).
- For Dieci Association Identities: above effect applies to Slash and Pierce Base Attack Skills as well; inflict 1 additional Defense Level Down.
If a Dieci Association Identity Discarded their own Skill using a Discard Skill, gain (rank of the Discarded Skill) Offense Level Up at the next Turn Start. (if multiple are discarded, take the highest value; max 3).
