- Increases Point Potency by 1 on the first line and Poise Count by 1 on the second line.

2 Poise Count
to all allies instead.

- Increases application on turn start to two allies, and adds 1 to Poise Potency. Increases Poise Count by 1 on the second line.
Turn Start: apply
3 Poise Potency to two random allies
without Poise Potency.
When all allies have Poise, apply
3 Poise Count
to all allies instead.
