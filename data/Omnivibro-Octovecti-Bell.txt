(If an Identity only has Attack Skills that gain Tremor Count on self, this E.G.O Gift does not activate for them).
When winning a Clash with a Skill that inflicts Tremor Potency or Count, or consumes Tremor Count on self: inflict (# of remaining Coins) Tremor Potency against the target; if the target does not have Tremor, inflict +3 Tremor Count.
Turn Start: gain (1 + (Tremor Count on self / 5)) Clash Power Up. (max 3).
When using a Skill that consumes Tremor Count on self: when defeating or Staggering a target, gain (Tremor Count consumed / 2) Tremor Count (once per Skill).
Skills that consume Tremor Count on self deal + (Tremor consumed on self + Tremor Potency on the enemy + 5)% more damage (except E.G.O Skills; max 50%).
